### Weapon item

This enumeration contains data that is useful to determine the weapon item kind

Each entry takes one byte in a memory, but in fact is stored as a part of a packed [ItemId](../../ALMFormat/ItemIdMeaning.md)

The following table describes all available values:

Id | Meaning
----|---------
 0x01 | BareHands
 0x02 | Dagger
 0x03 | ShortSword
 0x04 | LongSword
 0x05 | BastardSword
 0x06 | TwoHandedSword
 0x07 | Club
 0x08 | SpikedClub
 0x09 | Mace
 0x0A | MorningStar
 0x0B | PickHammer
 0x0C | WarHammer
 0x0D | Staff
 0x0E | ShamanStaff
 0x0F | Pike
 0x10 | Halberd
 0x11 | Lance
 0x12 | Axe
 0x13 | TwoHandedAxe
 0x14 | ShortBow
 0x15 | LongBow
 0x16 | Crossbow
 0x17 | Rem
 0x18 | SonicBeam
 0x19 | FlameThrower
 0x1A | BoulderThrower
 0x1B | PlasmaSword
